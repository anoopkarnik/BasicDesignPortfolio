Swamimalai, one of the abodes of Lord Sri Murugan, is situated on the northern banks of the river Cauvery, in Thanjavur district, Tamilnadu. It is a religious and pilgrimage centre besides being famous for bronze icons. Icons are the sacred images in temples, exist right from the time the temples came up. Here, in Swamimalai, both solid and hollow casting of these images is practised. But solid casting is by far the more ancient and important tradition in this centre.
